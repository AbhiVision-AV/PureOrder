PureOrder

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 29 May 2026

PureOrder values your privacy and is committed to protecting user information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the PureOrder application and related services.

By using PureOrder, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

PureOrder may collect the following information from users:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Delivery address
  • Location information
  • Google account information used for authentication

This information is collected only to provide app functionality and improve user experience.

How We Use Information

The collected information may be used to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Process and deliver food orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send important order-related notifications

Google Sign-In

PureOrder supports Google Sign-In for authentication. When signing in with Google, limited account information such as name and email address may be received from Google according to their authentication policies.

Location Information

PureOrder collects location information only for order delivery purposes. Location access is used solely to help deliver food orders to the correct delivery address.

Users can disable location access through device settings.

Notifications

PureOrder may send notifications related to:

  • Order updates
  • Delivery status
  • Important service announcements

Users can manage notification permissions through device settings.

Third-Party Services

PureOrder may use trusted third-party services including:

  • Google Firebase
  • Google Sign-In
  • OneSignal

These services may process limited information necessary for app functionality, authentication, analytics, and notifications.

Data Security

We use reasonable security measures and encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL) to help protect user information during transmission and storage.

Data Sharing

PureOrder does not sell personal user information to third parties.

Information may only be shared when necessary for:

  • Providing delivery-related services
  • Legal compliance
  • Fraud prevention and security
  • Trusted third-party services supporting app operations

Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data through the Delete Account option available in the Profile section of the app or by contacting support at contact.pureorder@gmail.com.

Children’s Privacy

PureOrder is a general audience food delivery application and is not specifically directed toward children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published within the app or website.
